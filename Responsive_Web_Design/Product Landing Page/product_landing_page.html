Suggestions for use

For the ultimate blending experience cut your fruit into pieces around 1.5cm x 1.5cm. We also advise you remove the peel and kernel of the fruit. Suitable for smoothies, protein shakes,
margaritas, baby formula, and even ice. Easy to clean; add water, a small drop of dishwashing liquid (if you want) and blend for 5 seconds then rinse out.
